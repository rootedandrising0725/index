✶ My Story

Built from pain, powered by faith

Hey mama, I’m Makayla — a 22-year-old born-again Christian wife and mom. At 21 I experienced my first pregnancy and then a devastating miscarriage in November 2024. One month later, I was pregnant again. My daughter was born in September 2025 — and went straight to the NICU.

Through pregnancy loss, a NICU journey, postpartum depression, breastfeeding struggles, financial strain, and marriage difficulties — I kept believing. Rooted & Rising was born from everything I walked through, because no young mom should walk it alone.
